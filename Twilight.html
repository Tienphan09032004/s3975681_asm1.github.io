Twilight

Author: Stephenie Meyer

Bella Swan, a 17-year-old girl who moves from sunny Phoenix to the gloomy town of Forks, Washington, to live with her father. Bella's life takes an unexpected turn when she meets the enigmatic and strikingly beautiful Edward Cullen at her new school. The book explores their forbidden romance as Bella discovers Edward's supernatural nature, making him a vampire with extraordinary abilities.
